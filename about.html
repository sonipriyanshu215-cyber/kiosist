About Kiosist

Bridging the gap between US hotels and round-the-clock staffing.

Kiosist Pvt. Ltd. was founded in Surat, India, with a singular mission: make professional front desk coverage accessible to every US hotel — not just the ones that can afford overnight in-person staff.

We pair our trained virtual agents with KioClerk — our proprietary kiosk and remote-agent platform — to deliver seamless check-ins, guest query resolution, and PMS management from across the globe.

Our Journey

From idea to 100+ hotels

Scroll through our story — one milestone at a time.

2020

The Idea

Rahil identifies a critical gap: US hotels struggling with overnight staffing costs and inconsistent front-desk coverage.

2021

Incorporated

Kiosist Pvt. Ltd. is founded in Surat, India. First team of 5 agents trained on US hospitality standards and PMS systems.

2022

First US Hotel Goes Live

We deploy KioClerk at our first US property. 24/7 virtual front-desk coverage powered from India — a first for the industry.

2023

50 Hotels Served

Growth accelerates as word spreads. 50 properties across 15 states now trust Kiosist for overnight and full-day coverage.

2024

45+ Team Members

Team triples in size. KioClerk v2 launches with enhanced kiosk integration, multi-PMS support, and real-time dashboards.

2025

100+ Hotels & Expanding

Over 100 hotels served across the USA. New verticals — motels, extended stays, and boutique hotels — join the platform.
